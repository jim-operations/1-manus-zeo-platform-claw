Key Findings from "Standardizing Zonal Education Offices" (Ministry of Education, Management and Quality Assurance Branch)

1. Published by the Management and Quality Assurance Branch, Ministry of Education
2. Minister of Education at the time: Akila Viraj Kariyawasam
3. Secretary of Education: Sunil Hettiarachchi

Key organizational divisions mentioned in the document:
- General and Establishment Management Division
- Education Administration Division
- Education Development Planning Division
- Finance Division
- Indicator Division (monitoring/evaluation)

The document emphasizes:
- Zonal education offices are regional administrative units managing limited resources for education
- They bring together teachers, educationists, in-service advisors, and administrators
- They act as mediators between schools and line/provincial ministries of education
- They are responsible for quality development of education to regional needs
- They implement education policies and new education reforms
- The document provides guidelines for standardizing the structure across all zones
- Aims to ensure equal rights to access education by minimizing resource distribution anomalies
- Includes an assessment process for zonal office performance

From the Negombo Zonal Education Office website, the branches include:
- Teacher Establishment
- General Administration
- Accounts Branch
- School Administration
- Education Development
